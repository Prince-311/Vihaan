Welcome to S C Unkal — your trusted kirana store in Akki Honda, Hubballi. We have been serving local families with pure, fresh edible oils and daily household essentials for over two decades.

We specialize in cold-pressed and premium-grade oils — sunflower, mustard, groundnut, palm, and coconut — sourced directly from trusted suppliers. No adulteration, no compromise.

Quality, fair prices, and a warm welcome — that's the S C Unkal promise to every Hubballi family.
